About Us

Based in Brazil, Joca Games brings to the market what is best in gaming industry. Founded in 2021, the company caught the world's eyes with Joca's Freeway and is gaining ground in the industry day after day.

Our mission is: "Provide moments of entertainment and remind the world that no matter how hard things are, there is always time to have fun. Woof! Woof!".

With a small but dedicated team, with extensive experience in game design, we strive to develop games with a high standart of excellence, seeking to create narratives that will engage the user and transport them to a new world.

Joca's Freeway

A retelling of the classic Freeway answering one of the most popular questions of all time: "Why did the pomeranian cross the road?" Incredibly optimized for the new generation, with bolder graphics and an extremely charismatic protagonist. You'll certainly have your heart in your mouth every time you try to cross the freeway.
